Luna Gabriela

Founder & Primary Instructor

About Luna

Luna Gabriela is the heart and soul behind Yoga by Luna. Her name reflects her Spanish heritage—"Luna" means "moon," symbolizing constant cycles of change and rebirth. This philosophy is woven throughout her teaching approach, embracing transformation as a natural part of our spiritual journey.

Originally pursuing Biomedical Sciences at university with a focus on anatomy and neuroscience, Luna made a profound transition from academic stress into wellness and healing work. This unique background brings a deep understanding of both the scientific and spiritual aspects of yoga practice.

Qualifications & Certifications

  • 200-hour Yoga Teacher Training (YTT) - Completed in India (2023)
  • Sound Therapy Practitioner - Healing through vibrational medicine
  • Certified Astrologist - Cosmic guidance and lunar wisdom
  • Life Coach - Personal transformation and growth support
  • Academic Background - Biomedical Sciences (Anatomy & Neuroscience)

Teaching Experience

Over the past 5 years, Luna has shared her wisdom and practice across multiple continents, including:

  • Australia - Retreat facilitation and workshops
  • India - Traditional yoga immersion and training
  • Sri Lanka - Spiritual practice and meditation teachings
  • United States - Community classes and private instruction
  • Mexico - Retreat leadership and cultural immersion

Teaching Philosophy

"Yoga is just the beginning of a healing and self-discovery journey. It can be the seed for CONNECTION—with yourself and with others."

Luna emphasizes yoga as union within body, mind, and spirit, positioning it as a practice that builds family, network, and human connection. Her approach combines traditional yogic wisdom with modern understanding, creating a bridge between ancient practices and contemporary life.

Specializations

Astro-Yoga

Integrating astrological wisdom with yoga practice, aligning movement and breath with cosmic cycles and lunar phases.

Sound Healing

Using vibrational medicine and sound therapy techniques to enhance healing and deepen spiritual practice.

Transformational Coaching

Guiding students through personal transformation using life coaching principles combined with spiritual practice.

Retreat Facilitation

Creating immersive experiences that combine yoga, meditation, cultural exploration, and personal growth.

Personal Journey

Luna's transition from the academic world to spiritual teaching represents her own journey of transformation and awakening. Having experienced the stress and pressure of conventional academic paths, she deeply understands the need for balance, healing, and authentic connection in our modern world.

Her multicultural teaching experience has enriched her understanding of diverse spiritual traditions and healing modalities, allowing her to offer a truly integrative approach to wellness and personal growth.
